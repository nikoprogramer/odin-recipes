Coffee recipe:

Description:

In this recipe you will master the art of making... instant coffee, that's the only one I drink :)

\

Ingredients:

  • Instant coffee
  • Sugar (if you like coffee with sugar)
  • Milk (if you like coffee with milk)

Steps:

  1. Boil some water
  2. Put 2 teaspoons of coffee into your favourite mug
  3. Pour the hot water into the mug with your coffee
  4. Add sugar and milk to your liking
